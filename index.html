Pediatric Surgical VR Headset Adapter

A 3D printable and sterilizable insert for Oculus headset use by children in the operating room, to help reduce the stress and fear children might experience pre-op. Made to fit multiple O2 mask sizes without interfering with surgery.

Tissue Adhering PVA Cardiac Patches

PVA patches applied to the heart after infarction. Meant to assist in remodelling without scarring. Tissue adhering on one side so as to not be attached with sutures but not stick to the pericardial sac.

Alginate-Pluronic Self Healing Hybrid Gels

Shear thinning Alginate-Pluronic hybrid gels. Intended for injection at a wound site since shear thinning. Due to the incorporation of pluronic the gel mechanically crosslinks and thermogels, healing and gelating after injection.

Evolutionary Robotics

In this course I created a number of different simulated robots and used several methods of evolution to improve their fitness at a given task.

Biosensing Rock Paper Scissors

The final project for my Biosesning and Signals course. Using machine learning I created and trained a system to read muscle contractions in the forearm via EMG and classify it as a rock, paper, or scissors gesture.

Implant Filter

The final project for my RF Circuit Design course. I designed and manufactured a bandpass filter for low power membrane implants.

BioFET Microfluidic Testing Jig

As a CAPSTONE project for Global Foundries I developed atestting rig for running microflows of biofluids over BioFETS. The design is quick to set up, opticaly accessible for use at a probe station, easy to manufacture, and capable of containing potentially hazardous biofluids.

Bioprinted Patches

As a final project for a Biomdical Engineering Lab class I designed and created different hydrogel patches on a 3D bioprinter. The mechanical integrity of composite patches with normal and hybrid gels was tested in shear vs just homogenous gels.

Speech to Text Mask

A costume piece that utilizes CMU PocketSphinx on a Raspberry Pi 3 with a throat mic in order to transcribe speech to a custom LED matrix in real time.

Lab Equipment Restoration

Most of my home lab equipment is either homemade or restored. Pictured here is a Stormer Viscosimeter I repaired and restored!
