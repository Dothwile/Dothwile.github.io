Multiplayer Tanks

Is a multiplayer cooperative strategy and shooting game where you and your friends face off against other tanks. Running a Client-Server Host architecture to play with anyone around the world. Eight different enemy tanks with different behavior and state based artificial intelligence.

Arcane

Venture into dungeons, slay monsters, and collect loot in this top down RPG. With all elements of the game designed using inheritance to quickly create customizable weapons, monsters and environments. As well as using behavior based Artificial Intelligence to create emergent behavior for all the characters in the game. All art hand drawn in 16bit pixel art with a limited pallet of 16 colors.

Tank

Tanks! is a top down combat game where the player maneuvers a small tank through multiple missions, trying to fight enemy tanks while avoiding enemy fire. There are multiple switch based AI's for the varied enemies. The player and the enemy tanks can also lay mines that interact with the environment. Tanks! is a mobile app and webgl app. Aiming is done with linear projections, and bullets ricochet with vector dot product math. Coded with c# and unity game engine.

Miracle

Is a first person adventure game where you venture into the wilderness to learn and study the magic of the world. While exploring you will have to fight off terrifying monsters and you will find mystical phenomena. Taking what you learned back home you can use that to research more complicated magics.

Virtual Reality Tower Defense Game

Is a Virtual Reality and leap motion project that utilizes your hands to control the map and path enemies take to try to get towards you as well as you can fire magical blasts from your hands to fight against the invading monsters.

By Lanterns Light

Is a 2D platformer about a cursed boy that must protect his lanterns’ flame and escape the Witches curse. Water is the natural enemy of the boy as well as harsh terrains that test his ability to navigate the world as he is pursued by the evil witch that cursed him. As the boy advances, he learns the true power of his lantern giving him multiple movement abilities that help him in his adventure.

TireLess

Is a 3d endless runner about a tire rolling down the road and must move out of the way and jump over obstacles, collect coins to get a high score. With procedurally generated obstacles and coins to create a unique gameplay every run.

Ascent

Is a 2D platformer in a world that has fallen into chaos and instability, and you’ve traced this madness to its source, a tower of magic. You must be brave to ascend the tower and save the world. The tower features many unstable elements you must learn to control in order to reach the top.
